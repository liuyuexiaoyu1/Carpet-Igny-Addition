## beacon

`/log beacon`

Real-time rendering of the beacon's range box.

- Smooth transition animations are displayed when the beacon's range changes, expands, or retracts.
- Large-scale rendering may cause slight frame drops on low-end devices.
- **Requires this MOD to be installed on the client side as well.**
